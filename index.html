About Me

Hello! My name is Laura Paul. I'm currently a senior Computer Science major at Arkansas Tech University in Russellville, Arkansas, but I am originally from Malvern, Arkansas. With encouragement from my aunts, sister, and dad who have had careers in programming and IT, I started seriously learning to code as a freshman in college, and I've learned a lot over the past few years from school, work, internships, and projects.

Through my courses, I have accrued experience in a variety of languages, frameworks, and platforms listed below, and developed a more thorough understanding of the logic and structure of computers and software design. In my work as a tutor, orientation leader, and youth development professional, I have taken on roles as a teacher and mentor, and learned how rewarding it is to share knowledge, advice, and an appreciation for learning with students and peers. These roles have also enhanced my communication and teamwork skills, and reinforced my grasp on the concepts I taught. An internship with the Arkansas GIS Office gave me experience working in an office setting, completing individual assignments and meeting with necessary coworkers to collect and share project requirements and plans. Collaborating on and leading group projects has refined my practical programming and project management skills while teaching me how to be adaptable and flexible to team and project needs and delegate tasks with consideration and efficiency.

In this variety of work, classes, and projects, I have come to enjoy all the levels of programming, from designing and creating front-end interfaces to building and managing back-end infrastructure. As I am completing my senior year, I'm looking forward to starting my career as soon as possible so I can apply what I have learned and continue building on my knowledge and expertise with people who can help me grow.
